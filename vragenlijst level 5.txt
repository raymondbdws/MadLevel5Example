 What are the parts that Android Architecture Components consist of?
activities, fragments, services, content providers and broadcast receivers.

Which design principle is followed by the Android Architecture Components?
separation of concerns and Drive UI from model?
 
What is the purpose of LiveData?
Monitor changes to objects without creating explicit and rigid dependencys paths between them. It also respects the lifecycle stat of the app component.

What is the purpose of a ViewModel?
Provides data for specific UI component eg. fragments or activities. It can be called from other components
